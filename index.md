Starting any piece of writing is incredibly difficult... Just like how every raindrop is more noticeable when it starts to rain, every word feels more weighty when you start to write. And when words feel more weighty, there's a natural disposition to be particular and precise to a fault - to overthink, which often leads to cryptic austerity.

So how do you write an introduction? I've tried analogies, theses statements, quotes, prose - and yet every time, I feel dissatisfied. Every introduction that I craft feels insufficient... Maybe it's because I'm not that well-written, maybe it's because of the ontological insufficiency of human writing, I don't know...

But I have come to realise that there's only one right way to start a piece of writing (or anything worth doing for that matter), only one thing that leaves me satisfied - to begin in the name of God.

<p dir="rtl" lang="ar">بسمِ اللهِ الرحمنِ الرحيمِ</p>

With the name of God, the one whose mercy is all-encompassing and continuously bestowed.

Appealing to divine invocation is my only reprieve from words that are fated to be eternally tainted with imperfection.

But what of this work? Well, I intend for this to be an assorted collation of all my writing and thoughts. 

Why should you read what I have to say? Maybe you shouldn't. I'm just a random person on the internet after all. 🤷‍♂️

So why does it exist? This mostly exists for my own benefit. I think that if I give form to my thoughts and beliefs, I'll be better able to identify the holes and inconsistencies in my worldview. Why are you here? I don't know, maybe you should ask yourself that question...

In any case, I would recommend extreme caution and skepticism when exploring this site.

I'm not a scholar lol. I'm just a guy.

السلام عليكم
